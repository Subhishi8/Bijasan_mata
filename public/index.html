About Bijasan Mata Mandir

Badi Bijasan Mata Temple is located in Sendhwa town of Barwani district of Madhya Pradesh, near the border of Madhya Pradesh and Maharashtra. This temple is an important place of Bijasan form of Maa Durga and is a center of faith for devotees from three states. In the temple, Mother Goddess is worshipped in the form of Pindi, which changes its form thrice a day. The temple is situated in the Satpura valleys, near the Maharashtra-Madhya Pradesh border. This temple is the Kuldevi (family deity) of many communities of Maharashtra, Gujarat and Nimar region, hence lakhs of devotees come here for darshan, worship and to fulfill vows.
